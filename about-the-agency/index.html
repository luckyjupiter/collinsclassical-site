About the Agency

Booking services and management for singers, musicians, and special performing acts.

Collins Classical provides booking services and management for their roster of singers, musicians and special performing acts.

Besides our fine international reputation, esteemed local clients in the Dallas/Fort Worth area have included Eatzis Market and Bakery, Adolphus Hotel, Enchant, The Mansion on Turtle Creek, Cox School of Business, Dallas Country Club, Park City Club, Fun Time Parties and Entertainment, Stone Briar Country Club, Ebby Halliday, Brierley Foundation, Fiesta Corporation, Bass Family, Dallas Symphony Guild, Royal Oaks Country Club, and many others.
