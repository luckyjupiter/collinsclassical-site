Collins Classic Carolers

A Dallas Tradition since 1985 - Go Local!

Elegant Victorian costumes

All your favorite holiday tunes

Beautiful classically-trained voices

You can have the nostalgic sounds of holiday carols at your special event as the Classic Carolers enhance your party ambience by singing your favorites as they stroll about. These carolers are the best voices in Dallas. Their splendid Victorian costumes of rich velvets, taffeta plaids for the ladies, frock suits and top hats for the gentlemen, give a festive appearance to any event.

Our quartets have delighted Dallas/Fort Worth Metroplex folks since 1985. Their holiday spirit in song has been enjoyed by Mary Kay, Inc., Adolphus Hotel, The Mansion, Neiman Marcus, Presbyterian Hospitals, KPMG, OmniAmerican Bank, IdeaRC Media, Brook Hollow Golf Club, City Club, Sabre Realty, Bent Tree Country Club, Gleneagles Country Club, and many private parties throughout the metroplex.
