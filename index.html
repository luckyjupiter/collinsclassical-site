Classical musicians and entertainment

With a focus on classical musicians, Collins Classical has represented artists from such esteemed companies as the Metropolitan Opera, La Scala, New York City Opera, Opera de Monte Carlo, San Francisco Opera, Glyndebourne, and orchestras of San Francisco, Los Angeles, Dallas, Pittsburgh, Boston, Philadelphia, Milan Symphonic, Santa Cruz de Tenerife, and Jerusalem.

The agency's associates are recognized for expertise in music, the best in talent, efficient administration, and, above all, integrity.
