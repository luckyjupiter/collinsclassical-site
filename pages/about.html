About Bluebonnet Music Productions

Four decades of connecting extraordinary musicians with extraordinary moments in Dallas-Fort Worth.

Collins Classical Entertainment Agency

Artists from the Met, La Scala, and major symphonies worldwide. Trusted by the Adolphus Hotel, The Mansion on Turtle Creek, Neiman Marcus, and dozens more.

Our Director

Jackie Lengfelder leads Collins Classical and Bluebonnet Music. Classically trained soprano. Personally matches every musician to every event.

Our Founder: Dr. Mary Ella Collins

Founded MECA in 1985. Nine seasons at the Metropolitan Opera. Ph.D. from UNT. Board member of The Dallas Opera, Dallas Symphony, and Dallas Theatre Center.

Mary Ella passed on to her heavenly home in September, 2020. Her legacy carries on.
